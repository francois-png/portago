FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What exactly is the Portago AI Concierge?
It's an AI assistant trained on a curated database of 3,000+ verified Mallorca businesses, services, and local knowledge across 18 categories. You ask questions in natural language and receive personalised, specific answers — with real names, addresses, prices, and insider tips. Think of it as a knowledgeable local friend available 24/7.
How is this different from ChatGPT or Google?
ChatGPT draws from general internet data and can hallucinate facts. Google gives you ten links and makes you do the research. Portago is trained on a hand-curated, verified local database that's updated daily. Every recommendation is backed by real data — not scraped reviews or outdated blog posts. You get one considered answer, not a page of search results.
When does it launch?
The AI Concierge is launching soon. Join the waitlist to be notified the moment it goes live. Early supporters will receive exclusive perks and priority access. The underlying directory of 3,000+ businesses is already live and growing daily.
Is it free to use?
There will be a free tier with a limited number of daily questions covering restaurants, activities, and basic area information. A premium tier will unlock unlimited questions plus deeper capabilities — property analysis, school comparisons, legal and tax guidance, construction advice, and personalised recommendations that learn your preferences over time.
How does it know all this information?
We've built a comprehensive database covering every major business category on the island — from restaurants and schools to lawyers, architects, and property agents. Each listing is verified by our team, enriched with local knowledge, and updated regularly. We combine this structured data with expert-contributed content on legal processes, tax requirements, area guides, and more.
Is the information accurate and up to date?
Yes. Our database is updated daily with the latest business information, opening hours, reviews, and pricing. For legal and tax guidance, we partner with local professionals to ensure accuracy. However, we always recommend confirming critical details (especially legal and financial) with a qualified professional — and the concierge can recommend the right one.
Can I use it before moving to Mallorca?
Absolutely — that's one of the most popular use cases. Many users start months before their move, using the concierge to research areas, compare schools, understand the property market, estimate costs, and plan their relocation step by step. It's available 24/7 regardless of your time zone.
What languages does it support?
The concierge supports English, German, Spanish, French, and Swedish — the five most common languages among Mallorca's international community. You can switch languages mid-conversation.
Can it replace a real estate agent or lawyer?
No — and it's not designed to. The AI Concierge is a research and guidance tool that helps you make more informed decisions and ask better questions. For transactions, contracts, and official filings, we always recommend working with qualified local professionals. The concierge can recommend the right ones based on your language, location, and specific needs.
I'm a local business — how do I get listed?
Basic listings are completely free. Visit our "List Your Business" page to claim or create your listing. Once listed, your business becomes discoverable through the AI concierge — meaning users searching for your type of service will see your name, details, and reviews. Premium and featured tiers are available for businesses wanting enhanced visibility.
Is my data private?
Yes. We don't share your conversations or personal data with third parties. Your chat history is encrypted and only used to improve your personal recommendations. You can delete your data at any time. We're fully GDPR compliant.
